Patternizer

Help

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your cursor.

Press Q to add a note if the selected slot is empty.

Press E to remove a note on the selected slot.

Press the spacebar to start/stop a song.

You can change the BPM (Beats Per Minute) using the slider above the arrow.

Press the square brackets [] to change the selected note's sample or pitch.

IMPORTANT! Make sure you have DESELECTED the BPM slider before moving your cursor, because Unity thinks you're trying to increase the BPM by moving left or right.

You can deselect an object by clicking another object in the game.
